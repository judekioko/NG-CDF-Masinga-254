About Masinga NG-CDF

Masinga Constituency is one of the eight constituencies in Machakos County, Kenya, created in 2009 from Yatta District. It is listed as constituency number 075 by the IEBC.

Geography

  • Area: Approx. 1,411 km²
  • Location: Masinga District, Machakos County
  • Administrative Wards: Kivaa, Masinga Central, Ekalakala, Muthesya, Ndithini
  • Sub-locations: Total of 28 sub-locations

Population & Demographics

  • Total Population (2019): 148,522
  • Gender Distribution: 73,519 Male, 75,001 Female, 2 Intersex
  • Age Groups:
    • 0–14 years: 54,655
    • 15–64 years: 84,100
    • 65+ years: 9,765

Education

  • 65% of residents have only primary education – the highest in Machakos County
  • 18% of residents have no formal education – nearly twice that of Mavoko Constituency

Economic Activities

The economy is primarily based on agriculture. Fertile lands support the cultivation of various crops for both subsistence and commercial farming.

Development & Infrastructure

  • The NG-CDF supports various community projects
  • Projects include school renovations, lab constructions (e.g., Mikuyuni Secondary), and classroom improvements (e.g., Makila Primary)

Political Representation

  • Current MP: Hon. Joshua Mbithi Mwalyo
  • MCAs: Each of the five wards has one Member of County Assembly
