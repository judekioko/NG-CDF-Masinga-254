Masinga Constituency Allocations (2013 - 2024)

Masinga Constituency has ensured that the funds disbursed from the board are utilized to develop infrastructure and projects that will benefit the people of Masinga and improve their lives. The constituency management further ensures that the residents participate fully in project proposals and launches. Local materials are sourced within the constituency when available.

Despite challenges such as drought and famine, the NG-CDF has played a key role in improving education through bursary awards for secondary and tertiary institutions, significantly reducing illiteracy levels.

In alignment with Kenya’s Vision 2030, Masinga Constituency has facilitated electricity installation in primary schools like Kanyonga Primary School and initiated borehole drilling to enhance agriculture.
