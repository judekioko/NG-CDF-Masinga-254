Our Achievements

Education Sector

  • 272 classrooms constructed to improve learning conditions and accommodate growing student populations.
  • 409 classrooms renovated to enhance the learning environment and extend the life of existing infrastructure.
  • 44 science laboratories constructed to promote practical learning in science subjects.
  • 198 pit latrines constructed to support better sanitation and hygiene in schools.
  • 11 dining halls constructed to support proper student nutrition and welfare.
  • 17 dormitories built to accommodate students, particularly those in boarding schools.
  • 55 administration blocks constructed to support school management and operations.

Security and Public Administration

  • 23 new offices constructed for security officers to enhance service delivery and community safety.
  • 41 pit latrines constructed for the security department.
  • 4 security offices fenced and fitted with gates to ensure secure premises.
  • 3 chiefs’ offices and 9 assistant chiefs’ offices constructed to support local administration.

Water and Sanitation

  • 3 boreholes drilled to improve access to clean and safe water for communities.

Community and Resource Development

  • 2 resource centers established to support learning and community empowerment initiatives.
  • 4 rural electrification projects implemented to enhance access to electricity in underserved areas.

Transport and Mobility

  • 3 school buses purchased to facilitate safe and reliable transportation for students.

Administrative Infrastructure

  • 1 NG-CDF office block constructed to support effective and efficient delivery of development programs in the constituency.

The residents of Masinga Constituency appreciate the progress made possible by the NG-CDF.
