Our Projects

Kamangulu Borehole (Environmental Conservation)

This project focuses on environmental conservation through the development of the Kamangulu Borehole to improve access to clean water in the area.

Kikumini Police Post (Improving Security)

We aim to improve the working conditions of our security personnel by enhancing the facilities at the Kikumini Police Post, ensuring safety for both officers and the public.

Masinga Constituency Public Participation

This initiative fosters greater community involvement by encouraging public participation in the decision-making process for various projects in the Masinga constituency.

Secondary School Dorms

Our secondary school dorms are designed to ensure comfort for the learners and to help enhance COVID-19 protocols by providing enough space for social distancing.

Modern Dormitory Interior

State-of-the-art dormitory interiors providing comfortable living spaces for students, featuring modern amenities and enhanced safety measures for optimal learning environment.

Bursary Cheques Issuance at Musingi Secondary School

Hon. Joshua Mbithi Mwalyo, Area Member of Parliament, personally issuing bursary cheques to deserving students at Musingi Secondary School. This initiative supports over 500 students across Masinga Constituency, ensuring access to quality education by covering school fees and educational materials. The program targets orphaned, vulnerable, and bright students from economically challenged backgrounds.

Bursary Program Ceremony at Musingi Secondary School

Area MP Hon. Joshua Mbithi Mwalyo presiding over the bursary issuance ceremony at Musingi Secondary School. The event witnessed the distribution of cheques totaling KSh 62 million to support secondary and tertiary education. The program emphasizes the NG-CDF's commitment to educational empowerment, with special focus on girl child education and students pursuing STEM courses at university level.

Best Practice Demonstration Program in Kimuuni

The CEO of NG-CDF Board leading a best practice demonstration program in Kimuuni, accompanied by Area MP Hon. Joshua Mbithi Mwalyo, committee members, and key stakeholders. This capacity-building workshop focused on project management excellence, financial accountability, and community engagement strategies. The program showcased Masinga's successful projects as national benchmarks for effective CDF implementation and sustainable community development.
